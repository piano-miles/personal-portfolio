Research

Publications & Work

Toward the Thermodynamic Limit: Neural Operators for Non-equilibrium Dynamics of Mott Insulators (Anandkumar Group)

2025 · Scientific Machine Learning · Preprint

Developed a Fourier Neural Operator to model nonequilibrium dynamics in strongly correlated systems. Trained on small lattices, the model demonstrates zero-shot generalization to systems exceeding 1024×1024, producing physically consistent predictions far beyond the tractable regime of conventional solvers.

Transformer-based Jet Reconstruction for Dark Matter Signatures (ATLAS / CERN, Whiteson Group)

2024–2025 · High Energy Physics · Research

Developed transformer-based models for reconstructing jet kinematics in simulated collider data. Investigated missing momentum signatures associated with dark matter production using a full simulation pipeline (MadGraph, Pythia8, Delphes) on GPU-enabled HPC systems.

Wavefunction Simulator

JavaScript · Numerical Methods

A real-time 1D solver for the time-dependent Schrödinger equation using a fourth-order Runge–Kutta integrator, allowing users to specify arbitrary potentials and simulate the resulting wavefunction dynamics.

2D Vehicle Dynamics Simulator

JavaScript · Physics Simulation

An interactive 2D sandbox for simulating vehicle motion under realistic force models, including acceleration, tire friction, and lateral slip, with emergent behaviors such as skidding.

Baryon Simulator

A classical particle system sandbox, modeling simplified baryon interactions using the Reid potential and attractive and repulsive electric forces, assuming aligned spins.

Computational Visualizations

Spectral Thin-Film Path Tracer

A real-time interactive Monte Carlo spectral path tracer rendering thin-film interference on a reflective glass sphere, featuring rapid sample convergence, ACEScg color management, and a custom lift/gamma/gain-inspired grading pipeline.

Orbital Volumetric Renderer

A real-time volumetric path tracer visualizing atomic orbital probability densities as participating media, with absorption and scattering behavior modulated by the wavefunction’s phase.

Stark-Shift Orbital Visualizer

An interactive 2D visualization of a hydrogen 1s orbital under a uniform external electric field, computed via time-independent nondegenerate perturbation theory and rendered with density gradients and contour lines.

Procedural Island Renderer

A real-time landscape renderer procedurally generating island terrains, featuring reflective and refractive water, dynamic wave motion, and atmospheric scattering effects.

Hydrogen Orbital Atlas

A real-time visualization presenting a grid of 2D slices of the hydrogen wavefunctions, designed for intuitive exploration and educational presentation of orbital structure.

Mandelbrot Surface Renderer

A real-time Mandelbrot set visualization with interactive lighting control, producing a shaded, quasi-3D appearance.
